Chapter 1 : Gothic Literature

[imaginaires : effrayantes]

What are you going to learn in this chapter?

  • You will learn about the gothic literature genre.
  • You will know some famous gothic stories.
  • You will learn vocabulary to express fear.
  • You will learn vocabulary to express like/dislike.
  • You will learn to recognize 5 literary devices and literary words in English.

What stories are you going to study?

  • Tale of the Ancient Mariner
  • Dracula
  • Frankenstein
  • Northanger Abbey
  • The Castle of Otranto

  1. The parody of a gothic novel

We have all the clichés of a gothic novel excpet for the really scary ones like death or the supernatural. Everything has a natural explanation. IT is just Catherine’s imagination that is playing with her mind.

  1. The narrator tells us what Catherine is feeling. She is an omniscient author. However, the observations of the author create distance between the scene and the reader. So we are not really afraid for Catherine.
